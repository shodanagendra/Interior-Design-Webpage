In interior design, services refer to the various types of professional work that designers offer to help clients improve, renovate, or decorate their residential or commercial spaces. Here’s a breakdown of common services in interior design:

---

🛋 1. Space Planning 

Designing the layout of furniture and fixtures to ensure functionality and flow.

Making best use of available space (especially in small rooms or offices).
---

🎨 2. Interior Styling / Decoration 

Choosing and arranging decor items like cushions, rugs, art, lamps, etc.

Enhancing the look and mood of a space through visual elements.
---

🏡 3. Residential Interior Design 

Designing interiors of homes, including:

Living rooms 

Bedrooms 

Kitchens

Bathrooms 


Personalized based on the client's lifestyle and preferences.
---

🏢 4. Commercial Interior Design

Designing for business spaces like:

Offices

Showrooms

Cafes

Retail stores


Focus on brand identity, functionality, and customer experience.
---

🧱 5. Modular Kitchen Design

Custom-designed kitchens with built-in cabinets, drawers, and optimized storage.

Focus on ergonomics and modern functionality.
---

🛠 6. Custom Furniture Design

Designing furniture pieces tailored to a space or client’s needs.

Can include wardrobes, TV units, study tables, sofas, etc.
---

💡 7. Lighting Design

Planning lighting layout for functionality and aesthetics.

Choosing the right type (ambient, task, accent) and placement of lights.
---

🧩 8. False Ceiling & Partition Design

Designing ceilings with lighting, texture, or panels (POP, gypsum).

Space-dividing solutions using wooden or glass partitions.
---

🏗 9. Turnkey Interior Solutions

"End-to-end" service where the designer handles the entire project:

Design

Material selection

Labor

Execution


Client gets a fully finished space ready to use.
---

🖼 10. 3D Visualization & Concept Design

Creating digital 3D models and renders of the design before execution.

Helps clients visualize the final result.
---

🧾 11. Consultation Services 

One-time or ongoing advice on design ideas, color schemes, furniture layout, etc.
Can be done online or in person.
